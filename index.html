About Me

To all of your pathetic little surprise, Miss Dee can actually cook quite well. She is a Southern woman who learned from her grandparents and great grandparents how to cook. Miss Dee enjoys shopping for more cookbooks, you can hold her bags behind, of course.
Miss Dee's Southern charm and quick, demmanding wit, and a lust to control and drain little subs, like you, is what makes her special. Miss Dee is truly an artistic blend and beauty. She gives you a retro-inspired atmosphere, elegance, and Motherly love -- with structure.
Miss Dee cooks, but you sit on the floor and wait for instruction to either tribute, or task. Tasks can involve showing me your pantry and ridding of expired foods, shopping, or cleaning chores.
