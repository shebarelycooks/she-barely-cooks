Kitchen Rules

  1. Age Verification (AV) is a MUST! This is non-negotiable.
  2. Silence during prep. Speak when spoken to. Obedience is expected in my kitchen.
  3. Weekly tribute every Sunday by 6pm CST. No excuses.
  4. Safe words: Yellow (slow)|Red (stop). Consent and boundaries are always honored.
  5. No refunds. Any Chargebacks or reversal results in a permanent ban from the kitchen.
  6. No illegal requests, no minors, no doxxing. All interactions are consensual, voluntary, and for entertaiment purposes only. Tributes are treated as gifts, never payments for services.
  7. Respect the kitchen. Disrespect earns chores, penalties, and my disappointment — and you don't want that.
